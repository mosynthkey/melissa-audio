Privacy Policy for Wavy

Last Updated: 2025-09-27

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in Wavy for iOS.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data recorded in Wavy for iOS.

The Wavy app allows you to select an image and an audio file from your device. These files are processed locally on your device to generate a video file. The original files and the generated video are only stored on your device. We do not have access to your files or the generated video.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect any personal information in our app.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services that would collect information about you.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly remove the information.
